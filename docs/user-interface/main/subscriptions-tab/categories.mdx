# Subscription Tab UI

<Img url="/img/ui/ui-main-subscriptions-01.png" maxHeight="500px" />

### 1. Category group

Imported accounts are categorized by their respective blockchain network. OpenGov networks with active referenda subscriptions are also listed under the __Subscriptions__ tab.

A category (or group) can be collapsed and expanded by clicking on its header.

### 2. Account name

Clicking on an imported account name will open its available subscriptions.

### 3. OpenGov group

Clicking on an OpenGov network will display its referenda with active subscriptions.
